Our History

Coffee has a long and inspiring story. It began over a thousand years ago in Ethiopia, where people first discovered the energizing taste of coffee beans. From there, coffee traveled to Yemen, Turkey, and Egypt, where the very first coffee houses were created.

Today, coffee shops are more than just places to buy coffee—they are warm, welcoming spaces where people come together to study, work, or spend time with friends.

At our coffee shop, we carry this tradition forward by serving quality coffee in a cozy environment that makes you feel at home.
